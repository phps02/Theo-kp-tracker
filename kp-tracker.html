Mitigation Protocol · When Kp Is Elevated
⚠ Moderate Alert · Kp ≥ 4.0 × 2 periods
Heightened Awareness
  • No visitors or new people in the home
  • Maintain strict feeding / medication schedule
  • Quiet environment — minimize arousal events
  • Extended grounding time (grass/soil, 20–30 min)
  • Verify evening magnesium glycinate dose is not missed
  • Log current Kp value in seizure diary
🔴 High Alert · Kp ≥ 5.0
Full Pre-Seizure Protocol
  • All Moderate actions plus:
  • Administer melatonin (1.5–3 mg, immediate release) that evening
  • No grooming, vet appointments, or boarding
  • No high-arousal play or exercise
  • Rescue medication accessible and confirmed
  • Confirm neurologist contact is available
Mechanism · Why geomagnetic storms affect seizure threshold
Geomagnetic storms suppress nocturnal melatonin via cryptochrome-mediated HPA axis activation. Suppressed melatonin lowers the threshold for hippocampal/amygdala seizure discharge propagation. The mitigation strategy targets this pathway directly — melatonin supplementation during storm windows, magnesium (NMDA antagonist) to block calcium flux, and stress minimization to avoid HPA co-activation. Source: Persinger et al. (Int J Biometeorology) · Kp data: NOAA SWPC (services.swpc.noaa.gov).
